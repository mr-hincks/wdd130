Choose your Adventure

Excitement and Adventure

Feel the ultimate adrenaline rush navigating wild rapids on the Volta River! This all-day adventure is perfect for experts or those eager to level up. Ideal for spring and summer, but thrilling year-round—an experience you won’t regret!

Family Adventure Float

A relaxing ride perfect for families with young kids! Enjoy scenic views, mild rapids, and a riverside picnic. Pass by the Cocoa Village Guest House—ideal for all experience levels. Make lasting memories with your loved ones on this adventure.

Eco Adventure Quest

A guided trip that focuses on the natural beauty and ecological significance of the Lake Bosomtwe. Learn about local wildlife and ecosystems while enjoying a moderately paced rafting adventure. This trip will take a full day and will take you through serveral towns along the river. Exploring is at the heart of this trip. Enjoy Nature with us!
